Alabaster Heart Foundation

We operate as a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our work is to advance charitable and public benefit initiatives that support community well‑being, with programs in community care, education & outreach, and emergency relief.

What We Do

Immediate care and long‑term opportunity for our neighbors.

  • Community Care: food & hygiene kits, navigation to services
  • Education & Outreach: classes, workshops, and resources
  • Emergency Relief: rapid aid for families in crisis

Community Care

Compassionate outreach and basic needs for vulnerable neighbors.

Education & Outreach

Workshops and materials that equip families to thrive.

Emergency Relief

Rapid assistance during displacement, disaster, or hardship.
